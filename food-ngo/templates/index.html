Food safety & dignity first

Why redirect food instead of tossing it?

Globally, tons of edible food is discarded while many people face hunger. Small acts—one tray from a party, extra catering, or tonight’s leftovers—add up when logistics are simple and safe.

Cut waste & emissions

Food in landfills produces methane. Rescue reduces footprint and honors the resources that grew your meal.

Support neighbors

NGOs distribute to shelters, community kitchens, and families—often the same week you donate.

Guided safety

We share temperature and packaging tips so donations stay wholesome from your kitchen to theirs.

How PlateForward works

Four quick steps—from your surplus to a verified NGO pickup. No complicated forms; we only ask what partners need to coordinate.

Donation tip Keep hot food hot and cold food cold; use sealed containers when possible. When in doubt, our partner will advise.
